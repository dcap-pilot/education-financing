Who is DCAP?

DCAP is a pioneering Malaysian-based financial technology platform dedicated to enable access financing for all. We believe that financial constraints should never limit a student's potential in the rapidly evolving AI and Tech landscape.

We partner with financial institutions to offer affordable financing to parents to fund your children's university journey. Beyond funding, DCAP ensures your child doesn't just get into university but also equip them with real career opportunities and exposure to regional AI & tech network, events, and entrepreneurial experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the role of DCAP in this program? expand_more

DCAP is the ecosystem builder bringing together the university, financing partner and career & tech industry players to offer this unique education financing program to parents.

DCAP will facilitate the application of parents and perform preliminary credit assessment. Final decision of approval will be based on the bank's discretion.

We will perform assessment for subsidy eligibility based on financial position of parents.

During the course of study, DCAP will provide students with exposure and experience with latest tech & AI developments and entrepreneurship through events, sharing and mentorship from seasoned entrepreneurs, funding opportunities by investors and hands-on job opportunities at DCAP's affiliated partners.

Who can apply for this program? expand_more

The program is open to Malaysian parents with children planning to enroll in pre-university, diploma and undergraduate courses at APU.

When can I start applying? expand_more

We now welcome registration for the pre-launch of the program before the launch of program in the 3rd week of April.

Our team will reach out to you to facilitate the application once it is launched.

The interest rate and subsidy will be on a first come first serve basis so we encourage you to register in the pre-launch.

Which courses can I qualify for? expand_more

We welcome students interested to study and build their career in tech, fintech and AI as follows. We also welcome registration of interest for other courses which will be considered on a case by case basis.

  • Diploma in ICT
  • Diploma in Mechatronic Engineering
  • Foundation Programs (non-Engineering & Engineering)
  • Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons) (Artificial Intelligence)
  • Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Computer Science (Cyber Security)
  • Bachelor of Mechatronic Engineering with Honours
  • Bachelor of Financial Technology (Hons)
What does the financing cover? expand_more

Unlike traditional loans, our coverage is comprehensive, including full tuition fees as well as necessary living expenses to ensure students can focus entirely on their studies.

How does this compare to existing financing products? expand_more

This programme is specifically designed for students entering this university. It not only provides financing to cover tuition fees and living expenses but also includes career access, industry exposure, and additional support not available in standard loans.

How will my child benefit from this network? expand_more

Students gain real-world exposure, mentorship opportunities, access to startup funding networks, and priority job opportunities.

How do I qualify for the subsidy? expand_more

The subsidy is designed to support families who may need additional financial assistance.

Eligibility is assessed based on your household financial situation during the application process.
