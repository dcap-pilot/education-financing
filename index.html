Who is DCAP?

DCAP is a pioneering financial technology platform dedicated to bridging the gap between ambition and opportunity. We believe that financial constraints should never limit a student's potential in the rapidly evolving AI and Tech landscape.

Our mission is to provide affordable, transparent financing solutions while fostering a direct pipeline to career opportunities. Through our ecosystem, we don't just fund degrees; we cultivate the next generation of digital leaders.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the role of DCAP in this program? expand_more

DCAP acts as the primary technology and coordination partner, managing the ecosystem that connects students with financing from our banking partners and educational opportunities at APU.

Who is the banking partner? expand_more

We are proud to collaborate with Alrajhi Bank to provide secure, Shariah-compliant financing options for our students.

Who can apply for this program? expand_more

The program is open to Malaysian citizens planning to enroll in approved undergraduate courses at APU. Eligibility depends on academic performance and financial assessment.

What does the financing cover? expand_more

Unlike traditional loans, our coverage is comprehensive, including full tuition fees as well as necessary living expenses to ensure students can focus entirely on their studies.

How does the 1.5% p.a. subsidy work? expand_more

Eligible families from lower-income brackets can receive an interest subsidy of 1.5% per annum, further reducing the financial burden of higher education.
